About the Service

BioInfoUni is a free, non-commercial, community-driven educational resource focused on bioinformatics, computational biology, biotechnology, and related academic programs.

The website aggregates publicly available information about universities, degree programs, courses, and educational opportunities from official university websites and other public sources in order to improve accessibility and discoverability for students and researchers.

BioInfoUni is an independent project and is not affiliated with any university, institution, or organization listed on this website. The source code of this project may be publicly available as part of the project's open-source and community-oriented nature.

Educational Information Disclaimer

BioInfoUni does not create, verify, sponsor, rank, accredit, or endorse universities, programs, courses, or institutions displayed on this website.

All information is provided for informational purposes only and may become outdated, incomplete, or inaccurate over time. Users are responsible for independently verifying all information directly with the relevant institution before applying, enrolling, making educational decisions, or relying on any information presented on this website.

Links to universities or external educational resources redirect users to third-party websites that are not controlled or operated by BioInfoUni.

Privacy & Data Collection

BioInfoUni is designed to minimize collection of personal data.

If you voluntarily submit information through forms on this website (for example when submitting a review or reporting corrections), submitted information may include personal data such as:

  • email address,
  • name,
  • organization or institution name,
  • other information voluntarily provided by the user.

Such information is used solely for reviewing submissions and corrections, communicating regarding submitted information, and operating and improving the service.

BioInfoUni does not sell personal data and does not intentionally share personal data with third parties except where technically necessary to operate the website and related infrastructure.

Some technical information may be processed automatically by hosting providers, analytics providers, or security systems, including IP addresses, browser metadata, and server logs.
